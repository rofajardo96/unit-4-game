* There are four crystals displayed on the page and a randomly generated number to the top left hand corner of them
* Each one of the crystals has a value between 1-12 that will remain the same until you win or lose the round
* The objective is to click on any combination of the different crystals in order to match their combined added values to the randomly generated number
* There is no limit to how many times you can use any crystal, but if your total score goes over the randomly generated number on the top left, you lose.
* Each time you win or lose, the random number on top and the value of each crystal automtatically changes

* Best of Luck!!!
